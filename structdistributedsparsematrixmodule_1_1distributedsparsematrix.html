Detailed Description

A datatype for a distributed CSR matrix. A CSR matrix stores locally a block of SparseMatrix types. That local CSR matrix is also divided up into blocks for sending/receiving purposes. This allows us to deal with non square slices of a matrix, and is also important for the 3D multiply algorithm.
